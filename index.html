What is LedgerSpy?

LedgerSpy is an AI-powered, air-gapped financial forensics platform built for internal auditors, fraud investigators, and compliance teams. It ingests raw transaction ledgers and applies multi-dimensional anomaly detection algorithms — entirely offline, with zero data ever leaving your machine.

Core Capabilities

📊
Benford's Law Analysis
Detects fabricated amounts by checking first-digit frequency distributions.
🕸️
Entity Relationship Mapping
Graphs inter-vendor transaction flows to surface circular trading rings.
🤖
AI Anomaly Scoring
Multi-layer ML model assigns risk scores based on timing, amount, and vendor patterns.
🔒
100% Air-Gapped
All processing is strictly local. No cloud API calls, no external servers, no telemetry.
📝
Automated Audit Reports
Generates court-ready forensic memos with findings, risk scores, and recommendations.
📂
CSV Ledger Integration
Import, search, filter, and enrich transaction data — then export the updated ledger.

The Story Behind LedgerSpy — A Note from Piyush

📌 PS — Piyush's Story

"Three years ago, I was a junior auditor staring at 80,000 rows of transactions in a spreadsheet, trying to find out where ₹2.4 crore had disappeared. It took me six weeks and three sleepless nights to trace it to a circular shell trading network — a pattern a machine could have caught in seconds.

That experience broke something in me — not my spirit, but my patience for tools that don't work. LedgerSpy is what I wish I had then: a system that doesn't just number-crunch, but actually thinks — flagging the 11 PM payments, the suspiciously round numbers, the vendor names that look legitimate but aren't.

This isn't just a dashboard. It's a forensic weapon for every auditor who has ever been handed a mountain of data and a magnifying glass. We built LedgerSpy so the next auditor doesn't need six weeks. They need six minutes."

— Piyush, Co-Founder & Lead Forensics Engineer
